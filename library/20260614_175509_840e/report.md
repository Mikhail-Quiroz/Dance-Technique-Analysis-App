### Top Corrections

**1. Lift your back leg so the line is level or oversplit — don't let it drop toward the floor.**  
*Why:* A dropped back leg collapses the line of the jump; the back leg should reach at least parallel to the floor for the leap to look balanced and powerful.  
*Drill:* Arabesque press-backs and split holds focusing on lifting the back leg above hip height — practise slow grand battements back with a 2-second pause at the top.
