### Top Corrections

**1. Stack shoulders over hips over your supporting foot.**  
*Why:* Tilting off the vertical axis is the most common reason a pirouette falls out — rotation can't be sustained off-axis.  
*Drill:* Quarter- and half-turn pirouettes focusing only on finishing in perfect balance, before attempting full rotations.

**2. Keep your working leg at hip height — don't let it drop as you turn.**  
*Why:* The à la seconde position must be maintained throughout the turn; a dropping leg shifts your center of mass sideways and collapses the line of the turn.  
*Drill:* Standing à la seconde holds at the barre: lift the working leg to 90° and hold for 5 s, focusing on the hip flexor maintaining height. 10 reps each side.
